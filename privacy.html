Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

✅ Show Tracker collects no personal information. Your data stays on your device and in your own iCloud account. Nothing is sent to us or any third party.

What Information We Collect

We collect no personally identifiable information (PII) of any kind. Show Tracker does not ask for, store, or transmit your name, email address, or any other personal data.

Data Storage

All data you enter — your show list, watched episodes, and preferences — is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account via NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore. This data is owned entirely by you and is never accessible to us.

iCloud Sync

Show Tracker uses Apple's iCloud key-value storage to sync your data across your own devices. This sync happens between your devices only, through your Apple ID. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Data

Show Tracker fetches show metadata (titles, episode lists, streaming providers, posters) from The Movie Database (TMDB). This is a read-only API call used to populate the app with show information. No personal data is sent to TMDB.

Analytics & Tracking

Show Tracker does not include any analytics SDK, crash reporter, or advertising framework. We do not track how you use the app.

Children's Privacy

Show Tracker is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date above. Since we collect no personal data, changes are expected to be infrequent.
