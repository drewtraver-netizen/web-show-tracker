Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Show Tracker ("the app") is a personal tool for tracking TV shows and movies on iPhone and iPad. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and how it's stored.

Data We Collect

Show Tracker does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not track you, and does not share your data with third parties.

Data Stored on Your Device & iCloud

All your show and movie data — including your watchlist, watched episodes, and favorites — is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. This data is:

  • Stored under your own Apple ID
  • Not accessible to us or anyone else
  • Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy

Third-Party Services

Show Tracker uses the following third-party services to fetch show metadata, episode lists, poster images, and streaming provider information:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — TMDB Privacy Policy

These requests are made when you search for or refresh a show or movie. No personally identifiable information is sent to these services.

Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns (page views, device types, referral sources). No personal data from the app is sent to Google Analytics. You can opt out via Google's opt-out tool.

Children's Privacy

Show Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not collect personal data from any users.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
