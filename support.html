Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a show?

Type the show title in the search box at the top and press Return. Tap the correct result from the list, then tap Fetch Episodes to add it to your library with all seasons and episodes.

What if multiple shows come up in search?

If there are multiple results, scroll through the list and tap the correct show, then press Fetch Episodes to add that specific one.

How do I track episodes I've watched?

Tap a show name in your list to expand its episode list. Tap any episode to toggle it between watched (checkmark) and unwatched. Your progress saves automatically.

What does the green dot mean?

A green dot next to a show means you have at least one episode you haven't marked as watched yet. No green dot means you're fully caught up on that show.

How do I add movies?

Use the Shows / Movies toggle at the top to switch to Movies mode, then search and add movies just like shows.

How do I get new episodes for a show I've already added?

Tap Refresh All Shows to pull in the latest episodes and metadata for every show in your list. This is useful when a new season drops.

How does iCloud sync work?

Show Tracker syncs your data through your personal iCloud account. It syncs automatically when your data changes, when the app opens, and when iCloud sends an external-change notification. It may take a moment to sync across devices.

How do I delete a show?

Tap the trash icon next to a show and confirm the deletion prompt. Shows are never deleted by a stray tap — confirmation is always required.

Where does show data come from?

Show Tracker uses The Movie Database (TMDB) for show metadata, episode lists, streaming providers, and poster images.

What iOS version is required?

Show Tracker requires iOS 16 or later. It runs on both iPhone and iPad.
